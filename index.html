Who I Am

Operations isn't overhead. It's the engine.

I'm an operations leader with 8 years of experience building high-performing teams at the intersection of fintech speed and regulated banking rigor. My career has taken me from Wells Fargo's branch operations to the fast-moving risk and escalations environments of Cash App and Afterpay.

What sets me apart isn't just that I manage operations it's that I investigate what's broken, design what should replace it, and build the team capable of executing it. I've led regulatory remediations under tight timelines, designed cross-functional compliance frameworks, and launched entirely new operational channels from scratch.

I'm energized by complexity. When a system fails, a regulation shifts, or a product creates unintended customer harm I'm the person who runs toward it.
